Please do this in Hanoi
Apr 2023 • Couples
As mentioned in the header, please do this tour when you're in Hanoi. Our guide was "Long" (pretty sure I've butchered the name). A guy with intimate knowledge of the city, the region and the food. He was patient enough to field all questions about the cuisine, the culture and everything in between. We are really glad that he was assigned to us.

Now about the tour itself. Make sure you're on empty stomach as the food itself is to die for and you'd wanna sample everything that's been planned. Wear a pair of good boots as there'll be a bit of walking, a loose pair of pants as there'll be a lot of good food.

Would recommend it to all who visit Hanoi.
