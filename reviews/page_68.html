A must for any Hanoi visit
A must do for your stay in Hanoi! Patrick was so friendly and knowledgeable and took our small group (three solo travellers) to a number of different places around the old quarter. Don’t be fooled by the idea of “tasting” lots of different foods - these are full sized dishes and you will finish your tour with your tummy full of yummy food.

Special mention for Patrick accommodating for the torrential downpour. We stayed at the first stop to see if the rain would subside and he offered a restaurant alternative as opposed to continuing the tour. When we said we would brave the rain he found us all ponchos and plastic bags to cover our shoes so we could continue.

Favourite dish: Summer rolls
Try if your brave: the “bug water” with your pancakes
